A mobile has a battery.

Every battery has a capacity, upperlimit and lowerlimit. The battery has a functionality charging and 
discharging, charging will increase the capacity and discharging will reduce the charge capacity in the 
battery.

The mobile has functionalities like call, chat, playvideo, playgames and mobileCharging. The functionality  
call, chat, playvideo, playgames, consume battery charge and whereas mobileCharging increase the battery  
charge.

Note: Battery charge cannot increase beyond upperlimit
	  Battery charge cannot decrease below lowerlimit
